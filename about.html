Our story

Born from a
real problem.

This company wasn't built in a boardroom. It started with a grandmother, a second home, and a system that was failing her — quietly, expensively, and every single year.

📷 Team photo

"My grandmother owned a second home. A beautiful place, but managing it was exhausting."

Every small issue meant phone calls, driving back and forth, waiting for plumbers, coordinating painters, chasing gardeners. Each service worked in isolation. Costs were high, problems repeated, and there was never a single person truly responsible for the whole system. Trust was fragile. Efficiency was zero.

So I approached it the way an engineer would.

Instead of treating property management as occasional interventions, I built a structured, year-round operating model. One coordinated system. One accountable team. Preventive maintenance instead of emergency fixes. Transparent accounting. Verified professionals. Continuous oversight.

The goal was simple: my grandmother should be able to stay at home, open her phone, and know that everything is handled — maintenance, guests, income, compliance — without stress, without surprises.

That solution became our company.

We at Pastore Real Estate have grown steadily because owners value three things: reliability, control, and preservation of their asset. We protect properties as if they were our own. Gardens are maintained before they deteriorate. Repairs are managed before they become structural damage. Guests are welcomed professionally. Financial performance is monitored continuously, not seasonally.

A house is not just a listing. It is a long-term asset that requires consistent attention. When managed properly, it generates income while increasing in value. When managed poorly, it deteriorates quietly.

We chose to build a system that prevents the second outcome.

Today, many families trust us with their homes because they want peace of mind — not just during peak season, but every single day of the year.

And that remains our standard.
